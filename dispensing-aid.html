Project

Material Dispensing Aid

Ergonomic tool for precise adhesive dispensing; rapid prototypes + test rigs.

Overview

Adjustable shop aid that clamps to a lab scale to securely hold various packs and rods during weighing and setup.

Designed to fit multiple scale geometries and to disassemble for cleaning with acetone. 3D printed in polypropylene for chemical compatibility and durability.
