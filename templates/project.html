{% if p.slug == 'cat-speaker' %}

Overview

Cat-shaped Bluetooth speaker focused on compact, clean packaging and basic consumer-electronics design. Enclosure modeled in Siemens NX and 3D-printed with tuned wall thickness for acoustic rigidity.

Electronics: single-cell Li-ion with TP4056 charging, PAM8403 class-D stereo amp, CA-6928 Bluetooth receiver, and a panel potentiometer for volume. Emphasized enclosure layout and wiring consideration.

Parts & Specs

Enclosure
Siemens NX, 3D-printed (PLA)
Receiver
CA-6928, Bluetooth module
Amplifier
PAM8403, 2×3 W class-D
Power
TP4056 charger + 3.7 volt LiPo battery, E-switch
Speaker
3–4″ car replacement driver
Controls
Power: E-switch, Volume: potentiometer

{% elif p.slug == 'haptic-glove' %}

Overview

Haptic glove with flex-sensor finger tracking and tuned vibrotactile feedback for natural VR touch.

ChatGPT said: Flex sensors on thumb/index/middle drive coin vibromotors with an exponential curve for nuanced touch. Arduino maps bends to haptics; evolved from breadboard to a soldered, wearable harness.

Parts & Specs

Sensing
Flex sensors on each finger to capture bend and grasp pose
Feedback
Vibration motors driven with an exponential intensity curve to mimic touch/hold in VR
Control
Arduino microcontroller maps sensor input to haptics in real time
Build
Prototyped on a breadboard, then soldered into a compact harness for durability

Adjustable shop aid that clamps to a lab scale to securely hold various packs and rods during weighing and setup.

Parts & Specs

Holder

Adjustability
Slot-and-fastener layout fits multiple scale widths
Material & Cleaning
Polypropylene (PP) for chemical compatibility; fully disassembles for acetone cleaning
